THE BEST FOOD YOU HAVE NEVER HAD:

Beef Wellington

beef Wel•ling•ton:

noun

a dish of beef, typically coated in pâté de foie gras, wrapped in puff pastry, and baked.

Where can you possibly go wrong with a decadent slab of beef encased in crispy puff pastry crust and sloshed over with gravy and mushrooms? (Well, if you are vegan, vegetarian, or hate beef, pastry, or pâté de foie gras, this dish could be very wrong for you. *clears throat*) I have only had if cooked to medium-well. However, in my online search for photos, I discovered many seem to prefer it still 'moo-ing'.

I have also not have the beef wrapped in pâté de foie gras, which is apparently a pâté of fat goose liver and usually truffles sometimes with added fat pork. I am not 'fancy' enough to know off the top of my head what that was, so I looked it up. You are welcome, as you probably not 'fancy' either. (Though you could be...I am making a lot of assumptions here) Can't say I am very sorry that part was missing. My husband recreates this jewel of a dish sometimes when I have been very good, as it does take a decent amount of time and preparation. Each morsel is worth of every minute you don't have making it.
